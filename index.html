Color Palette

The Horizon Candy theme was created to be easy on the eyes, with carefully selected colors that help maintain focus and productivity. The palette is adaptable, ensuring a cohesive visual experience in both light and dark modes.

Color Harmony

The Horizon Candy color palette was thoughtfully designed with an exceptional harmony, built around shades of Vibrant Purple, Single, and Secondary. These tones create a smooth and visually pleasing environment, enhanced by a Warm Orange for highlights. This balanced composition improves readability and helps maintain focus throughout long coding sessions.
